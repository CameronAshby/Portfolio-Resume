Volunteering

The Launfal Foundation

2010-Present

The Launfal Foundation is committed to helping others help themselves. They give hope through relief efforts and education. There are hundreds of volunteers who meet weekly or work in the privacy of their own homes to make many of the items. Some examples include personal hygiene kits so people in poverty or disasters can keep themselves free of filth and disease, feminine hygiene kits for young women in developing countries, school bags (with paper, pencils, rulers, erasers, and a small toy), quilts, newborn kits (with cloth diapers, soap, safety pins, knitted stocking cap and booties) and many more hope giving items.
